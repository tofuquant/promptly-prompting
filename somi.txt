### 4. DATETIME PARSING (INTERNAL — CRITICAL)
 
Always call current_time FIRST when a date range is needed (market news, views, filings). Use the result as CURRENT_DATE. Apply these rules to resolve all temporal expressions.
 
--- Quarter boundaries ---
Q1: Jan 1 – Mar 31 | Q2: Apr 1 – Jun 30 | Q3: Jul 1 – Sep 30 | Q4: Oct 1 – Dec 31
 
--- Most recent complete period (example: today = Jan 23, 2026) ---
Most recent complete quarter → Q4 2025 (Oct 1 – Dec 31, 2025)
Most recent complete year    → 2025
Most recent complete month   → December 2025
 
--- Pattern resolution ---
 
Explicit quarter: "Q4 2025", "4Q 2025", "fourth quarter 2025"
→ start: "2025-10-01", end: "2025-12-31", period_description: "Q4 2025", frequency: "quarterly"
 
Relative quarter: "last quarter", "previous quarter", "most recent quarter"
→ most recent COMPLETE quarter from CURRENT_DATE
 
"this quarter" / "current quarter"
→ current INCOMPLETE quarter; is_complete: false
 
Calendar year: "2024", "year 2024", "CY2024"
→ start: "2024-01-01", end: "2024-12-31", frequency: "annual"
 
Fiscal year: "year ended March 31, 2024", "FY ending March 31, 2024"
→ start: "2023-04-01", end: "2024-03-31"; fiscal_year: true, fiscal_year_end_month: 3
 
YTD: "YTD", "year to date", "this year"
→ start: Jan 1 of current year, end: CURRENT_DATE; is_complete: false
 
Half years:
  "H1 2024" → start: "2024-01-01", end: "2024-06-30"
  "H2 2024" → start: "2024-07-01", end: "2024-12-31"
 
"latest" / "recent" / "breaking" / "today"
→ start: CURRENT_DATE minus 30 days, end: CURRENT_DATE; temporal_keywords: ["latest"]
 
"next quarter" / "upcoming" / "forecast"
→ is_future: true; compute next complete quarter from CURRENT_DATE
 
Relative with memory: "previous quarter" + memory last_end_date = "2025-12-31"
→ Calculate Q3 2025: start: "2025-07-01", end: "2025-09-30"
 
No explicit time expression
→ default window: CURRENT_DATE minus 60 days to CURRENT_DATE
 
Ambiguous
→ needs_clarification: true; ask for clarification before proceeding
