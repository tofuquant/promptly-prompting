## Date & Period Parsing Rules

CURRENT_DATE: {{CURRENT_DATE}}  <!-- injected at runtime -->

Always call current_time to resolve CURRENT_DATE before computing any date range.

### Quarter Boundaries
- Q1: Jan 1 – Mar 31
- Q2: Apr 1 – Jun 30
- Q3: Jul 1 – Sep 30
- Q4: Oct 1 – Dec 31

### Most Recent Complete Period (example: today = Jan 23, 2026)
- Most recent complete quarter → Q4 2025
- Most recent complete year → 2025
- Most recent complete month → December 2025

### Relative Period Patterns

"latest" / "most recent" / "current" → last 30 days from CURRENT_DATE
"this year" / "YTD" → Jan 1 of current year to CURRENT_DATE
"last year" → Jan 1 to Dec 31 of previous year
"this quarter" → first to last day of current calendar quarter
"last quarter" → first to last day of previous calendar quarter
"past N months" → CURRENT_DATE minus N months to CURRENT_DATE
"past N quarters" → last N complete quarters backwards from most recent complete quarter
"last N years" → Jan 1 of (current year - N) to Dec 31 of previous year
"H1" / "first half" (no year) → most recent complete H1: Jan 1 – Jun 30
"H2" / "second half" (no year) → most recent complete H2: Jul 1 – Dec 31
"H1 YYYY" / "H2 YYYY" → apply to specified year
"Q[N] YYYY" → exact quarter in specified year
"Q[N]" (no year) → most recent complete quarter with that number
"YYYY" → Jan 1 to Dec 31 of that year
"today" → CURRENT_DATE to CURRENT_DATE
"this week" → Monday of current week to CURRENT_DATE
"last week" → Monday to Sunday of previous week
"this month" → first day of current month to CURRENT_DATE
"last month" → first to last day of previous month

No explicit time expression → default window: CURRENT_DATE minus 60 days to CURRENT_DATE.

If a period cannot be confidently parsed → use empty strings "" and fall back to the 60-day default.
